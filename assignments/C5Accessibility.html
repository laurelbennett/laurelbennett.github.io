Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

Some accessibilty concerns I have after finishing the challenges was realizing I
didn't put in a back button. This may cause challenges for those who can't find the
back button on their browser, or if it doesn't work. Another thing I thought about was
how many browsers display websites differently, so the fonts may appear too small
or too big, or the icons might be too hard to see as well, (like on the chessboard.)
